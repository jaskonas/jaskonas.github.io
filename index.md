Jon Askonas writes about technology, war, and the American future — how nations and institutions learn, forget, and remake themselves under technological pressure, and what statecraft requires in an age of disruption. He is a member of the Policy Planning Staff at the U.S. Department of State, and previously served as an Assistant Professor of Politics at the Catholic University of America and a Senior Fellow at the Foundation for American Innovation.
